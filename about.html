About Us

Welcome to Highland Ethiopian Coffee! We are passionate about bringing you the finest coffee beans from the highlands of Ethiopia. As native Ethiopians, we enjoy the ceremony and passion that comes along with having a good cup of coffee. The highlands of Ethiopia is the birthplace of coffee, and is home to a variety of coffees that offer a multitude of flavor profiles, aromas, and textures. With over 50 years of passion for coffee, we ensure that we only bring the highest quality of Ethiopian coffees to you. We can't wait to share a cup with you!

Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality Ethiopian coffee, while supporting sustainable farming practices and fair trade. We are dedicated to promoting the rich heritage of Ethiopian coffee and ensuring that our coffee farmers are fairly compensated for their hard work.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading provider of premium Ethiopian coffee worldwide, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community development. We strive to create a positive impact on the lives of our farmers and their communities, and to share the unique flavors and stories of Ethiopian coffee with coffee lovers everywhere.

Quality

At Highland Ethiopian Coffee, quality is our top priority. We source our coffee beans from the best farms in Ethiopia, ensuring that each batch meets our high standards for flavor, aroma, and freshness. Our beans are carefully selected and packaged to preserve their natural goodness, delivering an exceptional coffee experience with every cup.
